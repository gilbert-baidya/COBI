Partnership Opportunities

Corporate Partnerships

For Businesses

Align your corporate social responsibility goals with our mission. We offer various ways for businesses to engage:

  • Financial Sponsorship: Fund specific programs or projects
  • Employee Giving: Matching gift programs and payroll deductions
  • Volunteer Programs: Organize employee volunteer opportunities
  • In-Kind Donations: Contribute products, services, or expertise
  • Cause Marketing: Joint campaigns that benefit both organizations
Explore Corporate Partnership

Foundation & Grant Partnerships

For Foundations

We welcome partnerships with foundations and grant-making organizations that share our vision:

  • Program Grants: Support for specific health, education, or economic initiatives
  • Capacity Building: Funding for organizational development and infrastructure
  • Research & Innovation: Pilot new approaches to community development
  • Multi-Year Commitments: Sustained support for long-term impact
Discuss Grant Opportunities

Faith-Based Organizations

For Churches & Religious Groups

Partner with us to extend your ministry and compassion to Bangladesh:

  • Mission Partnerships: Support spiritual development programs
  • Church Sponsorships: Adopt a community or specific project
  • Mission Trips: Organize service trips to Bangladesh
  • Prayer Partnerships: Regular prayer support for our work
  • Resource Sharing: Share materials, expertise, and networks
Connect Your Ministry

Educational Institutions

For Schools & Universities

Collaborate on educational initiatives and research:

  • Student Projects: Service learning and capstone projects
  • Research Partnerships: Academic research on development issues
  • Student Volunteers: Internships and volunteer opportunities
  • Awareness Campaigns: Campus events and fundraising
  • Knowledge Exchange: Faculty expertise and professional development
Start an Educational Partnership

Government & NGO Partnerships

For Public Sector & NGOs

Collaborate with us to expand program reach and effectiveness:

  • Co-Implementation: Joint program delivery and service provision
  • Resource Sharing: Combine expertise and infrastructure
  • Policy Advocacy: Work together on systemic change
  • Knowledge Sharing: Best practices and lessons learned
