Where We Work

Our programs serve communities across Bangladesh

Focus on Bangladesh

Channel of Blessings concentrates its efforts in Bangladesh, a developing nation where millions face challenges in accessing healthcare, education, and economic opportunities. Our work spans rural villages to urban communities, reaching those who need it most.

  • Adult literacy programs

  • School construction projects
  • Agricultural training programs

Rajshahi Division

Khulna Division
